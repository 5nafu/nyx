builds -
  Helper scripts for deb and rpm builds.
  
  For the debian build see 'deb-prep.sh'. At release time:
  - bump the version:
    - /resources/deb-prep.sh
    - /release/src/version.py
  - dump /release/ChangeLog into /resources/build/debian/changelog
